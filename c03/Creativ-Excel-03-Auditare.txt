CE FACE NANO BANANA / GEMINI IMAGEN 3
Este motorul Google de generare imagini foto-realistice. Genereaza imagini
de pana la ~2048x2048 px (sau 1920x1280 pentru aspect 3:2). Functioneaza
cel mai bine cu prompturi in engleza, lungi, detaliate, cu instructiuni
foto-tehnice specifice (lens, aperture, lighting, composition).

CUM SE FOLOSESTE
1. Acceseaza Gemini (gemini.google.com) sau aplicatia Banana 2 daca o ai
   instalata.
2. Selecteaza modul "Generate image" sau "Imagine".
3. Copiezi promptul intreg din acest fisier (de la "Cinematic editorial..."
   pana la "no cartoon style.").
4. Astepti generarea (10-30 secunde).
5. Daca tool-ul ofera 4 variante, alegi cea care respecta cel mai bine
   compozitia ceruta.
6. Salvezi imaginea la rezolutia maxima oferita.

REGULI ESENTIALE LA GENERARE
- NU modifica prompturile (au fost calibrate cu zeci de iteratii).
- DA, generezi mai multe variante daca prima nu este perfecta.
- Pastreaza paleta Trainity stricta: negru + alb + galben #FFD400.
- Verifica compozitia inainte sa salvezi: subiectul cheie trebuie sa fie
  in zona indicata (vezi notele de compozitie per prompt).

DUPA GENERARE
- Salveaza imaginile cu numele exact indicat in capul fiecarui prompt.
- Pune-le in folderul `assets/` alaturi de fisierele HTML.
- Daca livrezi fisier standalone (cu imagini incorporate base64), trimite
  imaginile la Claude pentru integrare automata.

VERIFICARE CONFORM (checklist universal aplicabil la fiecare creativ)
Inainte sa accepti o imagine ca finala, raspunde DA la urmatoarele 8:
1. Subiectul cheie este in pozitia ceruta?
2. Lighting-ul este DOAR auriu/galben warm (zero alte culori)?
3. Fundalul este in shadow profund (negru complet pe partea cu textul)?
4. Aspect ratio este orizontal (3:2 sau 16:9, NU patrat sau vertical)?
5. NU exista oameni, fete, maini in imagine (decat daca explicit cerut)?
6. NU exista text gibberish (litere aleatoare sub forma de "text fake")?
7. NU exista logo-uri vizibile sau brand text recognoscibil?
8. Imaginea simte ca apartine SERIEI Trainity (continuitate cu celelalte
   imagini deja generate pentru C03)?

Daca pierzi un singur DA: regenereaza cu prompt-ul identic. Modelul produce
variante diferite la fiecare rulare; alegi cea care trece checklist-ul.

SECTIUNEA 1 · COVER PAGE CU HOOK VIDEO YOUTUBE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CONTEXT
Aceasta este IMAGINEA THUMBNAIL pentru video-ul YouTube al constructiei C03.
Trebuie sa atraga click-ul. NU este o imagine cinematica subtila. Este
HOOK BRUTAL pe paradoxul vizibilitatii: ce pare curat NU este curat.

NUME FISIER SALVAT: `cover-yt-excel-03-auditare.jpg`
DIMENSIUNE THUMBNAIL YT: 1280x720 px (16:9 orizontal)
LOC IN PRODUCTIE: Thumbnail YouTube + cover pentru landing pages.


COMPOZITIE CERUTA
- Element principal centru-stanga: o pagina printata cu un tabel impecabil
  la prima vedere - randuri uniforme, coloane drepte, cifre curate. Sub
  o lampa cu fascicul warm yellow, devin vizibile insertii fluorescente:
  spatii fantoma la finalul cuvintelor, caractere zero-width ca puncte
  ghost, cifre stocate ca text revelate prin aliniere asimetrica.
- Dreapta (zona text in 45% latime negru profund): spatiu pentru titlu
  alb si subtitlu galben suprapuse in post-productie.
- Lighting: lumina laterala calda peste pagina pe stanga, restul scenei
  inchis. Aer cu particule fine ca un laborator forensic de noapte.


PROMPT COMPLET (copiaza intreg in Banana 2):

"Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2 aspect ratio, 1920x1280 px. Subject:
a single printed spreadsheet page resting on a dark forensic examination
desk. The table on the page appears clean at first glance with perfectly
aligned rows, straight columns, well-formatted numbers. But under a soft
warm yellow examination light raking across the page, faint fluorescent
markings reveal hidden contaminants: invisible trailing spaces glowing
as small dots at the end of words, zero-width characters as ghost marks
between letters, numbers stored as text betrayed by left-alignment, dates
written as strings exposed by inconsistent format. The contamination is
invisible to the naked eye but the forensic lighting makes it visible.

Composition: page positioned on the LEFT half of the frame, occupying
about 55 percent of width, centered vertically. RIGHT side, 45 percent
width, is deep black shadow reserved for typography overlay. The page
is lit by a single warm directional light source from upper-left, giving
it texture, paper grain, slight shadow depth. The dark side fades to
near absolute black.

Atmosphere: forensic laboratory at night. Fine dust particles in the
light beam. The mood is investigative, quiet, methodical, not dramatic,
not panicked. A specialist examining evidence.

Color palette: strict. Only black, warm white for paper, warm yellow
gold for highlights and fluorescent markings at #FFD400 tone, faint
violet hint in deep shadows. No blue, no green, no red. The yellow
markings should feel like discovered evidence, not decorative.

Technical: shot on a full-frame camera, 50mm prime lens at f/4, shallow
depth of field on the page edges, ISO 400, slight film grain. Reuters
forensic documentary aesthetic. Photorealistic, no illustration style,
no cartoon style."

face C03 fara sa citeasca text.

"Ah, C03 ia un tabel care pare curat, il trece printr-un audit invizibil
si demonstreaza ce contaminari ascundea fara sa schimbe valoarea reala
a datelor."
- Stanga (35 percent): tabel aparent curat - pagina printata cu randuri
  aliniate, cifre frumos formatate, ce pare verificabil.
- Centru (30 percent): scanner forensic orizontal - o bara luminoasa
  galbena trece peste pagina, lasand in urma marcaje fluorescente pe
  celulele contaminate (spatii fantoma, caractere ascunse).
- Dreapta (35 percent): raport forensic - pagina noua alaturata care

  modificate.
- Lighting: warm yellow accent peste scanner si raport, restul scena
  in shadow profund.


PROMPT COMPLET:

"Cinematic editorial infographic photograph, 3:2 aspect ratio, 1920x1280
px. Three-element horizontal composition representing data forensic audit:

LEFT, 35 percent width: a printed spreadsheet page that appears
immaculately clean - rows perfectly aligned, columns straight, numbers
well-formatted, dates uniform. The viewer's first impression is that
everything is fine.

CENTER, 30 percent width: a horizontal forensic scanning bar of warm
yellow light at #FFD400 tone passing across the page. Where the beam
crosses, faint fluorescent yellow marks appear on specific cells:
trailing space dots at word endings, ghost characters between letters,
asymmetric alignment indicators on number cells. The beam reveals what
was invisible.

RIGHT, 35 percent width: a forensic report page placed beside the
original, showing a structured summary with category icons and counts.
The text is suggested but never readable as real words. The report
visually implies contamination detected, values intact. A small warm
yellow stamp or seal in the corner suggests certification.

A subtle horizontal flow line connects the three elements suggesting
sequence: appearance, scan, proof. No arrows, no logos, no text.

Atmosphere: forensic laboratory desk seen from above. Warm desk lamp
from the upper-right, fine particles in the air, paper texture visible.
The mood is methodical, quiet, almost surgical - the precision of an
analyst documenting evidence.

Color palette: strict. Black background, warm white papers, warm yellow
gold at #FFD400 highlights for the scanner beam and fluorescent
markings, neutral gray for paper shadows. No blue, no green, no red.

Technical: top-down shot, 35mm lens at f/5.6, even depth of field across
all three elements, ISO 200, slight film grain. Editorial photojournalism
aesthetic. Photorealistic, no illustration style, no cartoon style.
Maintain visual continuity with other scenes in the C03 series of
forensic examination, fluorescent markings, evidence presentation."

NOTE DE PRODUCTIE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PALETA STRICTA C03
- Negru fundal (#000000)
- Alb cald hartie (~#F5F1E8)
- Galben Trainity (#FFD400) - accent unic, folosit doar pentru sigilii,
  marcaje fluorescente, beam-uri si highlight-uri
- Violet subtil in shadows (sugestie UV forensic, NU dominant)
- ZERO albastru, ZERO verde, ZERO rosu

SEMNATURA VIZUALA C03
- Pagina printata + marcaje fluorescente galbene = semnatura recurenta
- Lumina laterala calda directionala = lighting recurent
- Particule fine in beam = atmosfera recurenta
- Sigiliu galben in colt = certificare forensic

ORDINEA DE PRODUCTIE RECOMANDATA
1. Genereaza intai SECTIUNEA 3 (cele 6 imagini executive show) - formeaza
   universul vizual al constructiei C03.
2. Apoi SECTIUNEA 2 (infograficul) - reuseste cu paleta deja stabilizata.
3. Ultima SECTIUNEA 1 (cover YouTube) - varianta cea mai brutala, asculta
   semnatura vizuala dar amplifica contrast.

DUPA INTEGRARE BASE64
- Imaginile cinematice (sectiunea 3) se incorporeaza inline in
  HTML-Video-Excel-03-Auditare.html ca base64 (executive show).
- Infograficul (sectiunea 2) ramane in folder assets/ si se referentiaza
  prin path relativ in HTML-Studiu.
- Cover YouTube (sectiunea 1) NU se include in HTML, se foloseste DOAR
  pe YouTube si pe landing-uri.
