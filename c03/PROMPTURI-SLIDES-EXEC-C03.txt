CONTEXT
Aceste sase imagini apar in EXECUTIVE SHOW la finalul HTML-VIDEO al C03.
Fiecare e slide-ul de incheiere al unei etape. Toate sase TREBUIE sa
traiasca in acelasi univers vizual (continuitate de serie obligatorie).

LOC IN PRODUCTIE: HTML-Video, sectiunea Executive Show. Se salveaza ca
exec-stage-1.jpg ... exec-stage-6.jpg in c03/assets/ si se integreaza base64.

UNIVERS COMUN AL SERIEI C03
- Decor recurent: birou forensic intunecat, hartie examinata, lumina
  laterala calda directionala, particule fine in aer.
- Semnatura vizuala: pagina cu tabel + marcaje fluorescente galbene.
- Paleta stricta: negru + alb cald hartie + galben #FFD400 + violet subtil
  in shadows (UV forensic). ZERO albastru, verde, rosu.
- Aspect 3:2 orizontal (1920x1280), text slide-ului peste zona intunecata.

REGULA DE CONTINUITATE (obligatorie in fiecare prompt)
"Maintain strict visual continuity with the other scenes in the C03 series
of forensic spreadsheet examination, fluorescent contamination markings,
evidence documentation - same surface, same lighting, same paper, same mood."

NOTA RESCOPE V50: etapa 5 STABILIZARE = audit REPETABIL ca verificare, NU
flux care curata singur (mecanismul permanent refreshabil apartine C04).

ETAPA 01 · REALITATE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

NUME FISIER SALVAT: exec-stage-1.jpg   (1920x1280 px, 3:2)

CONCEPT: Aparenta de curatenie. Pagina printata cu tabel impecabil la prima vedere, lumina calda peste ea, fara niciun marcaj de contaminare inca vizibil. Operatorul recunoaste ca aparenta nu e dovada.

PROMPT (copy-paste integral in Banana 2 / Gemini Imagen):

"Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2 aspect ratio, 1920x1280 px. Subject:
a single printed spreadsheet page on a dark forensic desk. The table is
perfectly clean to the naked eye - rows aligned, numbers formatted,
dates uniform. No contamination is visible. The page looks trustworthy.

Composition: page positioned left-center, occupying about 55 percent of
frame width. Right 45 percent is deep black shadow reserved for
typography overlay. Page is lit by single warm directional light from
upper-left, revealing paper texture and subtle grain without showing
any flaws.

Atmosphere: forensic laboratory at the moment before examination begins.
Quiet, methodical, expectant. The viewer should feel that this looks
fine, but wonder if it really is. Fine dust particles in the light beam.

Color palette: strict black, warm white for paper, warm yellow gold
accents on the lamp glow. No fluorescent markings yet - this is the
before state. No blue, no green, no red.

Technical: 50mm lens at f/4, shallow depth of field on page edges,
ISO 400, slight film grain. Reuters forensic documentary aesthetic.
Photorealistic, no illustration style, no cartoon style. Maintain
visual continuity with other scenes in the C03 series of forensic
examination, fluorescent contamination markings, evidence documentation.
Same surface, same lighting style, same paper type, same mood."

ETAPA 02 · INVESTIGATIE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

NUME FISIER SALVAT: exec-stage-2.jpg   (1920x1280 px, 3:2)

CONCEPT: Auditul incepe. Un scanner luminos galben trece peste pagina. Pe celulele atinse de fascicul apar marcaje fluorescente. AI raporteaza, datele raman neatinse.

PROMPT (copy-paste integral in Banana 2 / Gemini Imagen):

"Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2 aspect ratio, 1920x1280 px. Subject:
a printed spreadsheet page being scanned by a horizontal beam of warm
yellow forensic light. Where the beam crosses the page, fluorescent
yellow markings reveal themselves on specific cells: small dots at the
end of words for trailing spaces, ghost characters between letters for
zero-width spaces, left-alignment indicators on number cells for numbers
stored as text, date-format inconsistencies.

Composition: page positioned center-right, occupying about 55 percent of
frame width. Left 45 percent is deep black shadow reserved for
typography overlay. The yellow scanning beam crosses horizontally at the
visual middle of the page, brighter where it touches problematic cells.

Atmosphere: forensic laboratory in active examination. The scanner beam
is the dominant light source. Fine particles drift through the beam.
The mood is methodical investigation - discovery happening in real time,
without drama, without panic. The page is being read, not modified.

Color palette: strict black background, warm white paper, warm yellow
gold at #FFD400 for the scanner beam and fluorescent contamination
markings. Subtle violet hint in deep shadows suggesting UV examination
wavelength. No blue, no green, no red.

Technical: 50mm lens at f/4, sharp focus on the scan area, slight motion
blur suggesting beam movement, ISO 400, film grain. Reuters forensic
documentary aesthetic. Photorealistic, no illustration style, no cartoon
style. Maintain visual continuity with other scenes in the C03 series
of forensic examination, fluorescent contamination markings, evidence
documentation. Same surface, same lighting style, same paper type,
same mood."

ETAPA 03 · TRANSFORMARE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

NUME FISIER SALVAT: exec-stage-3.jpg   (1920x1280 px, 3:2)

CONCEPT: Power Query ca audit engine. O succesiune vizibila de pasi PQ documentati pe o pagina alaturata, fiecare pas neutralizand o categorie de contaminare. Datele raman aceleasi, doar invelisul lor devine curat.

PROMPT (copy-paste integral in Banana 2 / Gemini Imagen):

"Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2 aspect ratio, 1920x1280 px. Subject:
two printed pages side by side on a forensic desk, showing data audit
transformation.

LEFT page, 45 percent width: the original contaminated spreadsheet,
fluorescent yellow markings still visible on problem cells - trailing
space dots, ghost characters, left-aligned numbers, string-formatted
dates.

RIGHT page, 45 percent width: a documented audit recipe page, showing
a list of forensic neutralization steps in structured format. Each line
implies a Power Query transformation step, visually rendered as
monospaced text blocks never readable as real words, each with a small
warm yellow checkmark beside it. The neutralization is demonstrated, each
contamination removed without touching the real value.

Between the two pages, 10 percent center width: a soft warm yellow light
gradient suggesting flow from contamination to clean, but no arrow,
no logo, no explicit symbol.

Atmosphere: forensic analyst's documentation moment. The contamination
has been catalogued and demonstrably neutralized. The mood is methodical
proof: showing that each contamination can be removed without changing the
real value. The permanent reusable flow belongs to the next stage, not here.

Color palette: strict black background, warm white papers, warm yellow
gold at #FFD400 for the fluorescent markings on the left page and for
the checkmarks and gradient on the right. No blue, no green, no red.

Technical: top-down shot, 35mm lens at f/5.6, even focus across both
pages, ISO 200, slight film grain. Editorial photojournalism aesthetic.
Photorealistic, no illustration style, no cartoon style. Maintain
visual continuity with other scenes in the C03 series of forensic
examination, fluorescent contamination markings, evidence documentation.
Same surface, same lighting style, same paper type, same mood."

ETAPA 04 · VERIFICARE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

NUME FISIER SALVAT: exec-stage-4.jpg   (1920x1280 px, 3:2)

CONCEPT: Conservarea sumei demonstrata. Doua pagini cu totaluri identice asezate alaturat - INPUT contaminat si OUTPUT curat. Suma martor egala pe ambele. Dovada ca operatia a curatat invelisul fara sa modifice valoarea reala.

PROMPT (copy-paste integral in Banana 2 / Gemini Imagen):

"Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2 aspect ratio, 1920x1280 px. Subject:
two printed spreadsheet pages placed side by side on a forensic desk,
each with a highlighted summary total at the bottom. The two totals are
visually identical - same digits, same font, same warm yellow accent
highlight box around them.

LEFT page, 45 percent width: the contaminated input version, with faint
fluorescent markings still visible on problem cells.

RIGHT page, 45 percent width: the clean output version, identical layout
but with all contamination removed. Numbers properly right-aligned,
dates in uniform format, text cells clean.

Between the two pages, 10 percent center width: negative space with
subtle warm yellow ambient glow. No equals sign, no arrow, no logo.

Both summary totals are highlighted with warm yellow gold at #FFD400
box accents. The visual proof is unmistakable: the value is conserved.

Atmosphere: the moment of forensic certification. Quiet, methodical,
conclusive. The mood is earned trust - the work is done, the proof is
on the desk.

Color palette: strict black background, warm white papers, warm yellow
gold at #FFD400 accents on the highlighted totals and faint contamination
markings on the left page. No blue, no green, no red.

Technical: top-down shot, 35mm lens at f/5.6, even focus, ISO 200,
slight film grain. Editorial photojournalism aesthetic. Photorealistic,
no illustration style, no cartoon style. Maintain visual continuity
with other scenes in the C03 series. Same surface, same lighting style,
same paper type, same mood."

ETAPA 05 · STABILIZARE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

NUME FISIER SALVAT: exec-stage-5.jpg   (1920x1280 px, 3:2)

CONCEPT: Auditul ca verificare repetabila (NU flux care curata singur). Pe un export nou, acelasi scan forensic se re-ruleaza ca sa CONFIRME daca datele sunt curate. Langa el, foaia-dovada certificata cu suma martor stabila ramane reper. Mecanismul permanent de curatare apartine C04, nu acestei etape.

PROMPT (copy-paste integral in Banana 2 / Gemini Imagen):

"Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2 aspect ratio, 1920x1280 px. Subject:
a freshly exported spreadsheet page on a dark forensic desk being
re-examined by the same warm yellow forensic scanning beam, depicting the
audit being RE-RUN as a verification check on new data, not an automated
cleanup.

CENTER-RIGHT, about 55 percent width: the new export page. The horizontal
warm yellow forensic beam passes across it again, the way it did in the
first investigation, testing whether contamination is present. A few faint
fluorescent markings light up where the beam finds issues, confirming the
audit still detects what is hidden.

LOWER-LEFT: the original certified proof sheet resting beside it, with a
small warm yellow seal and a highlighted summary total that stays stable
and unchanged, serving as the reference witness. The rest of the frame is
deep forensic shadow.

Between them: a subtle warm yellow ambient glow, but no arrows, no logos,
no explicit symbols.

Atmosphere: disciplined verification. The audit is repeatable - it can be
re-applied to any new export to confirm whether the data is clean. This is
not a machine cleaning by itself; it is a forensic check holding the proof
steady. The mood is methodical confidence, evidence re-confirmed.

Color palette: strict black background, warm white papers, warm yellow
gold at #FFD400 for the scanning beam, fluorescent markings, and seal.
Faint violet hint in the deepest shadows. No blue, no green, no red.

Technical: top-down slight angle shot, 35mm lens at f/5.6, even focus,
ISO 200, slight film grain. Editorial photojournalism aesthetic.
Photorealistic, no illustration style, no cartoon style. Maintain visual
continuity with other scenes in the C03 series of forensic spreadsheet
examination, fluorescent contamination markings, evidence documentation.
Same surface, same lighting style, same paper type, same mood."

ETAPA 06 · CONFIRMARE

CONCEPT: Predarea bazei demonstrabile catre C04. Pagina curata C03 cu sigiliu de certificare, langa o pagina noua goala cu titlu implicit. Continuitate cap-coada in cadrul aceluiasi univers vizual.

PROMPT (copy-paste integral in Banana 2 / Gemini Imagen):

"Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2 aspect ratio, 1920x1280 px. Subject:
two printed spreadsheet pages on a dark forensic desk, depicting the
moment of handoff between two stages.

LEFT page, 50 percent width: a completed audit-certified spreadsheet.
All contamination is gone. In the bottom-right corner is a small warm
yellow gold stamp or seal rendered as an abstract circular yellow mark,
no readable text inside it, signifying forensic certification. The page
projects authority, finality.

RIGHT page, 50 percent width: the top of a new spreadsheet just placed
beside the first. It is mostly blank, with only a faint header suggesting
the next stage of work begins. The header area is empty, ready to be
filled. The page suggests fresh production work, not a continuation of
the same.

Between the pages: minimal negative space. The two pages touch but do
not overlap. A subtle warm yellow ambient glow connects them.

Atmosphere: the closing of one chapter, the opening of the next. Quiet,
deliberate, no celebration - the mood of a forensic specialist signing
off and stepping away from the desk, leaving evidence prepared for the
next stage.

Color palette: strict black background, warm white papers, warm yellow
gold at #FFD400 for the certification stamp and ambient glow. No blue,
no green, no red.

Technical: top-down shot, 35mm lens at f/5.6, even focus across both
pages, ISO 200, slight film grain. Editorial photojournalism aesthetic.
Photorealistic, no illustration style, no cartoon style. Maintain
visual continuity with other scenes in the C03 series. Same surface,
same lighting style, same paper type, same mood."
