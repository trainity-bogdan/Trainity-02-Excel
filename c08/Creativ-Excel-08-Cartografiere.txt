CE FACE NANO BANANA / GEMINI IMAGEN 3
Este motorul Google de generare imagini foto-realistice. Genereaza imagini
de pana la ~2048x2048 px (sau 1920x1280 pentru aspect 3:2). Functioneaza
cel mai bine cu prompturi in engleza, lungi, detaliate, cu instructiuni
foto-tehnice specifice (lens, aperture, lighting, composition).

CUM SE FOLOSESTE
1. Acceseaza Gemini (gemini.google.com) sau aplicatia Banana 2 daca o ai
   instalata.
2. Selecteaza modul "Generate image" sau "Imagine".
3. Copiezi promptul intreg din acest fisier (de la "Cinematic editorial..."
   pana la "no cartoon style.").
4. Astepti generarea (10-30 secunde).
5. Daca tool-ul ofera 4 variante, alegi cea care respecta cel mai bine
   compozitia ceruta.
6. Salvezi imaginea la rezolutia maxima oferita.

REGULI ESENȚIALE LA GENERARE
- NU modifica prompturile (au fost calibrate cu zeci de iteratii).
- DA, generezi mai multe variante daca prima nu este perfecta.
- Pastreaza paleta Trainity stricta: negru + alb + galben #FFD400.
- Verifica compozitia inainte sa salvezi: subiectul cheie trebuie sa fie
  in zona indicata (vezi notele de compozitie per prompt).

DUPA GENERARE
- Salveaza imaginile cu numele exact indicat in capul fiecarui prompt.
- Pune-le in folderul `assets/` alaturi de fisierele HTML.
- Daca livrezi fisier standalone (cu imagini incorporate base64), trimite
  imaginile la Claude pentru integrare automata.

VERIFICARE CONFORM (checklist universal aplicabil la fiecare creativ)
Inainte sa accepti o imagine ca finala, raspunde DA la urmatoarele 8:
1. Subiectul cheie este in pozitia ceruta (centru-dreapta sau alta zona
   specifica per prompt)?
2. Lighting-ul este DOAR auriu/galben warm (zero alte culori)?
3. Fundalul este in shadow profund (negru complet pe partea unde sta
   textul)?
4. Aspect ratio este orizontal (3:2 sau 16:9, NU patrat sau vertical)?
5. NU exista oameni, fete, maini in imagine (decat daca explicit cerut)?
6. NU exista text gibberish (litere aleatoare sub forma de "text fake")?
7. NU exista logo-uri vizibile sau brand text recognoscibil?
8. Imaginea simte ca apartine SERIEI Trainity (continuitate cu celelalte
   imagini deja generate pentru C08)?

Daca pierzi un singur DA: regenereaza cu prompt-ul identic. Modelul produce
variante diferite la fiecare rulare; alegi cea care trece checklist-ul.

SECȚIUNEA 1 · COVER PAGE CU HOOK VIDEO YOUTUBE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CONTEXT
Aceasta este IMAGINEA THUMBNAIL pentru video-ul YouTube al constructiei C08.
Trebuie sa atraga click-ul intr-o oceana de thumbnail-uri concurente.
NU este o imagine cinematica subtila. Este HOOK BRUTAL.

C08 este ultima constructie din T2 CUNOASTERE. INTRIGA este:
"Te uiti la un tabel intreg. Jumatate din el e in alta parte."
Cover-ul trebuie sa exprime vizual harta relationala a setului - tabelul
central este legat prin fire de lumina de tabele-satelit din jur (CLIENTI,
PRODUSE, AGENTI, DEPOZITE), iar unul dintre satelit ramane orfan, neconectat.

NUME FISIER SALVAT: `cover-yt-excel-08-cartografiere.jpg`
DIMENSIUNE THUMBNAIL YT: 1280×720 px (16:9 orizontal)
LOC IN PRODUCȚIE: Thumbnail YouTube + cover pentru landing pages.


COMPOZIȚIE CERUTĂ
- Element principal CENTRU: o foaie imprimata cu un tabel de date (setul
  principal, Vanzari), asezata pe o suprafata intunecata. Coloanele de
  coduri (cod_produs, client) sunt evidentiate.
- Element-cheie: in jurul foii centrale plutesc 4 foi-satelit mai mici
  (nomenclatoare: CLIENTI, PRODUSE, AGENTI, DEPOZITE), fiecare la o
  distanta usoara, ca niste noduri ale unei retele.
- Mecanism: din coloanele de coduri ale tabelului central pornesc fire
  fine de lumina aurie catre foile-satelit corespunzatoare, ca niste
  chei de legatura care isi gasesc destinatia. Firele sunt clare,
  trasate, ca pe o harta.
- Element shock: una dintre foile-satelit ramane ORFANA - niciun fir nu
  ajunge la ea, e in shadow, usor estompata (satelit fara cheie comuna).
- Zona dreapta-sus: lasata libera, in shadow, pentru OVERLAY TEXT mare in
  post-productie ("UNDE TRAIESTE RESTUL?" scris in YouTube Studio).
- Iluminare dramatica de sus, lumina alba clinica pe tabelul central +
  rim galben warm (#FFD400) pe firele de legatura si pe sateliti.
- NU este aceeasi paleta moale ca slide-urile executive - este THUMBNAIL,
  trebuie sa sara la ochi.


PROMPT (copy-paste integral in Banana 2 / Gemini Imagen)

Cinematic editorial photograph, ultra-detailed, photorealistic 8K quality,
thumbnail style, high impact composition.

Subject: A central printed business data table laid flat on a dark
surface, photographed at a slight three-quarter angle, with code columns
clearly visible. Around the central sheet, four smaller satellite paper
sheets float at a slight 3D depth, arranged like nodes of a network, each
a reference table. Thin precise beams of golden light run from the code
columns of the central table out to the matching satellite sheets, like
keys finding their destination, drawn clearly like routes on a map.

One of the four satellite sheets is left ORPHANED in shadow, slightly
faded, with no beam of light reaching it, suggesting a source with no
common key.

Lighting: Hero spotlight from above creating dramatic contrast. The central
table is lit with cool clinical white light. The golden connecting beams
and the satellite sheets are warmed by amber light (#FFD400) from
above-right, casting subtle warm shadows onto the surface. Strong rim
light on the satellite sheets separating them from the dark background.

Composition: Subject occupies left two-thirds of frame. Right third is
left intentionally in deep shadow, available for overlay text in post-
production. Slight three-quarter angle camera position, like an editorial
investigation photograph.

Background: Matte black dark wood or brushed dark surface, slight grain
texture visible. No competing elements, no other props.

Color palette: Strictly monochromatic dark background with warm amber
yellow (#FFD400) accents on the connecting light beams and satellite
sheets. White paper, black printed text on the tables. No other colors.
The beams should glow slightly warm against the cooler central table.

Mood: Relational clarity, network mapping, "the most important data
about a row lives somewhere else". Conveys the moment a lone table
is revealed as a node in a larger system.

Style references: Editorial photography from Wired magazine forensic
features, product photography for premium instruments, chiaroscuro of
Caravaggio. Investigation aesthetic.

Technical: Shot on medium format camera, 50mm lens equivalent, f/4
aperture for shallow depth of field with the central table and connecting beams in
sharp focus and the satellite sheets slightly soft. No digital artifacts. Numbers
on the data table can be illegible blurred financial figures, NOT
gibberish letters.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 horizontal, 1280 by 720 pixels.

Negative prompt: no people, no faces, no hands, no text overlays added
post-process, no readable brand text, no logos, no other colors except
amber and the natural monochrome, no cluttered background, no fantasy
or sci-fi elements, no cartoon style, no category label cards, no
magnifying glass, no glowing orbs, no holograms, no world map, no globe.

SECȚIUNEA 2 · INFOGRAFIC ÎNȚELEGERE CONSTRUCȚIE ÎN 10 SECUNDE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CONTEXT
Aceasta este IMAGINEA RESUMAT care apare in landing page-uri, in pitch
deck-uri, in postari LinkedIn. Audienta trebuie sa inteleaga din priviri:
"Ah, C08 transforma un tabel necunoscut intr-un dictionar al setului,
profil complet, prin Copilot + Excel."

NUME FISIER SALVAT: `infografic-excel-08-cartografiere.jpg`
DIMENSIUNE: 1920×1080 px (16:9 orizontal)
LOC IN PRODUCȚIE: Landing page B2C/B2B, slide-uri pitch, LinkedIn posts.


COMPOZIȚIE CERUTĂ
- Stanga (35% latime): TABEL NECUNOSCUT - pagina printata cu un tabel de
  date care arata curat structural (datorita lui C01-C05 anterior), dar
  toate cifrele coloanelor sunt vagi sau acoperite cu o ceata gri.
  Iluminata difuz, mai rece. Operatorul vede randuri, dar nu intelege
  taxonomia.
- Centru (30% latime): TRANZIȚIA - o sageata aurie sub forma de fascicul
  de cifre galbene care zboara de la stanga catre dreapta, fiecare
  cifra clarificand o coloana ("CLIENT 15", "JUDET 8", "CATEGORIE 4",
  "MONEDA 3", "CANTITATE 12"). Acesta este "motorul de profilare".
- Dreapta (35% latime): DICTIONAR COMPLET - pagina printata sub forma
  unui dictionar / fisa de profilare: lista de coloane cu cifre clare,
  numere de cardinalitate vizibile, distributii top-3 marcate cu bare
  galbene. Iluminata cu lumina aurie warm (#FFD400), aratand claritate.
- ZERO TEXT pe imagine - infograficul este DOAR VIZUAL. Textul va fi
  adaugat in post-productie peste.
- Atmosfera: laboratorul unui taxonomist, NU diagrama tehnica plictisitoare.


PROMPT (copy-paste integral)

Cinematic editorial photograph, ultra-detailed, photorealistic 8K quality,
storytelling triptych composition.

Subject: A three-zone visual narrative composition on a single dark
surface, photographed from a slight overhead angle (45-degree top-down).

LEFT ZONE (occupying about 35% of image width): A printed business data
table page that appears structurally clean (no chaos, no torn elements)
but visibly opaque - column headers are covered by a soft gray fog
making them unreadable, suggesting columns exist but their taxonomy
is not yet known. Lit by cool diffuse neutral light, slightly desaturated.

CENTER ZONE (occupying about 30% of image width): The transition. A
flowing stream of small rectangular paper labels in warm amber tone,
floating from left to right across the dark surface, like a swarm of
golden butterflies organizing themselves. Each label shows simple
category text and a number ("CATEGORY 4", "REGION 8", "CLIENT 15",
"QUANTITY 12"). Volumetric haze and faint dust particles catch the
amber light. Strong directional energy.

RIGHT ZONE (occupying about 35% of image width): A second printed page,
this one styled as a clean dictionary or taxonomy sheet - rows of column
names paired with their distinct value counts in bold, small horizontal
bar charts showing top-3 distribution percentages, the page clearly
organized as a profile reference. Lit by warm amber light from above-
right, the paper catching a golden glow on its edge.

Lighting: Two-zone lighting story. LEFT side: cool diffuse neutral light,
slightly clinical and unflattering, with subtle gray fog over the headers.
CENTER: dominant warm amber stream (#FFD400) of flying labels. RIGHT:
warm amber accent light embracing the dictionary page from above-right.
The lighting itself tells the story of transformation from fog to clarity.

Composition: All three zones on a single continuous dark surface, no
visible borders or framing between them - the transition feels organic,
like a single visual story flowing left-to-right. Both papers angled
slightly inward toward the center stream, creating subtle visual tension
pointing toward the labeling moment.

Background: Matte black dark wood or brushed dark surface, identical
texture across the entire composition. No competing elements.

Color palette: Monochromatic dark scene, with the LEFT zone slightly
cooler (very subtle blue-gray tint, almost imperceptible, with light
gray fog), CENTER and RIGHT warmed by amber yellow (#FFD400). White
paper, black printed text. Strictly no other colors.

Mood: Operational discovery, taxonomic precision, narrative clarity.
Like a forensic laboratory at the moment of identifying every specimen.
Conveys: "unknown / labeling / known" without any text.

Style references: Editorial photography from Wired magazine science
features, product photography for premium tools, chiaroscuro of Caravaggio.
Maintain visual continuity with the rest of the C08 series.

Technical: Shot on medium format camera, 35mm lens equivalent for wider
field, f/8 aperture for sharper depth across all three zones, top-down
slight angle. No digital artifacts. Numbers on both papers can be
illegible blurred figures except the labels in the center stream which
remain readable.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 horizontal, 1920 by 1080 pixels.

Negative prompt: no people, no faces, no hands, no text overlays added
post-process, no arrows drawn as graphic elements (the labels stream IS
the arrow), no logos, no readable brand text, no other colors except
subtle cool gray and warm amber, no cluttered background, no fantasy or
sci-fi elements, no cartoon style, no infographic-style flat icons, no
charts or graphs drawn over.
