UNIVERS COMUN AL SERIEI C08
- Decor recurent: workspace negru mat, document-tabel alb central, sateliti
  (carduri document = entitati business), retea de linii amber care leaga.
- Arc: tabel singur -> sateliti apar -> chei identificate -> harta completa
  -> context viu -> predare catre C09 (modelul relational).
- Paleta stricta Trainity: negru + alb document + amber #f4b400 + gri neutru.
  ZERO dashboard, ZERO Power BI/Power Query, ZERO oameni, ZERO UI software.
- Aspect ratio 3:2, cinematic editorial photograph, photorealistic 8K.

EXEC-STAGE 1 · SETUL ESTE SINGUR
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Cinematic editorial photograph, ultra-detailed photorealistic 8K.

A large Excel-style business table printed on white paper sits isolated in the center of a matte black workspace. The table is perfectly clean and structured, but it stands completely alone. Around it, faint empty connection points and disconnected paths fade into darkness. No relationships exist yet. No surrounding entities are visible.

A subtle amber glow begins to emerge from the far distance but has not reached the table.

Composition emphasizes isolation.

Color palette strictly follows Trainity:

black background
white document
amber accents (#f4b400 style)
neutral gray shadows

Mood:

The data exists, but its world does not.

Aspect ratio 3:2.

Negative prompt:

no people, no faces, no laptops, no dashboards, no charts, no Power BI, no Power Query, no refresh icons, no arrows, no logos, no text overlays.
