CUM SE FOLOSESTE

2. Copiezi promptul integral (sectiunea respectiva, blocul PROMPT).
3. Generezi, descarci. Trimiti imaginea in chat la motor (Claude).
4. Motorul scoate watermark-ul Gemini (steluta din colt, R-V49.1) si o pune
   in c09/assets/ cu numele standard, apoi o integreaza base64 in HTML-Video.

DIRECTIA VIZUALA C09 (obligatorie pe toate imaginile)
C09 NU este C08. C08 = harta descriptiva a ecosistemului (cine cu cine s-ar
putea lega). C09 = ACTIVAREA relatiilor: surse separate care se conecteaza
prin chei, noduri, poduri, intr-un model care devine interogabil.

TREBUIE sa apara, vizual:
- surse de date separate (tabele/coli ca obiecte fizice distincte)
- chei / conectori / poduri care se cupleaza intre tabele (1:M)
- o relatie care se ACTIVEAZA (cheie care intra in lacas, linie care se aprinde)
- model curat, ordonat (star schema: un fact central, dimensiuni in jur)
- o singura intrebare care traverseaza tabelele si primeste raspuns

NU trebuie sa apara (interdictii dure, altfel imaginea e gresita):
- harti de ecosistem difuze tip C08, paienjenis de context descriptiv
- inventar / liste descriptive (teritoriul C05-C08)
- dashboard-uri, grafice publicabile, KPI vizuali (= T4)
- clasamente, top/bottom, Pareto (= C11)
- sageti de cauzalitate / "de ce", insight final (= C12)
- butoane de actiune, alerte, automatizari (= T5)
- oameni generici de business fara rol, stock photo decorativ

STIL VIZUAL TRAINITY
Cinematic editorial, foto-realist, paleta premium intunecata (matte black,
grafit, bleu-otel rece) cu un singur accent cald (amber/auriu) pe relatia
activa. Lumina dramatica, profunzime de camp mica, contrast inalt. Fara text
suprapus, fara UI de aplicatie, fara cartoon. 50mm, f/2.0, lumina laterala.

SECTIUNEA 1 · HERO (COVER) · axa RELATII
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SCOP PEDAGOGIC
Prima imagine pe care o vede cursantul. Trebuie sa comunice instant saltul
T2 -> T3: de la date cunoscute dar izolate, la un model legat care raspunde.
Intruchipeaza AHA: "Fara relatii ai date. Cu relatii ai raspunsuri."

DESCRIERE VIZUALA
Cateva placi/coli de date separate, suspendate pe un fond intunecat, fiecare
distincta (un fact central mai mare, cateva dimensiuni mai mici in jur). Intre
ele, conectori metalici lucrati ca niste chei/poduri care tocmai s-au cuplat:
liniile de legatura se aprind in amber dinspre fact spre fiecare dimensiune
(relatii 1:M). Un singur fascicul subtire de lumina pleaca dintr-o intrebare
si traverseaza doua-trei placi, intorcandu-se cu un punct luminos (raspunsul).

ELEMENTE OBLIGATORII
- 1 placa "fact" centrala + 3-4 placi "dimensiune" in jur (star schema citibila)
- conectori-cheie cuplati, linii 1:M aprinse in amber
- un fascicul-intrebare care traverseaza placile
RELATIA CU AHA
Placile izolate = "date". Conectorii aprinsi + fasciculul care se intoarce cu
raspuns = "raspunsuri". Contrastul izolat/legat ESTE mesajul.

ELEMENTE INTERZISE
harta difuza C08; dashboard; grafice; clasamente; text pe imagine; UI Excel.

PROMPT (copy-paste integral in Banana 2 / Gemini Imagen 3)
Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2, ~1920x1280. On a matte black studio
surface, several separate slabs of data float in space: one larger central
slab (the fact) and three or four smaller satellite slabs around it
(dimensions), each a clean brushed-steel panel etched with faint rows. Between
them, precision machined key-connectors have just clicked into place; glowing
amber relationship-lines ignite from the central slab outward to each
satellite, forming a clean star pattern (one-to-many). A single thin beam of
cold blue light departs from a small engraved question mark, travels across two
of the slabs, and returns carrying one bright amber point of light (the
answer). Dramatic side lighting, shallow depth of field, high contrast, premium
graphite and steel-blue palette with a single warm amber accent on the active
relations. 50mm, f/2.0. Photorealistic, no text overlay, no application UI.

NEGATIVE PROMPT
diffuse spider-web ecosystem map, descriptive context cloud, dashboard, charts,
bar graphs, ranking, leaderboard, KPI tiles, arrows of causation, alert icons,
buttons, generic business people, stock photo, cartoon, 3d render toy look,
cluttered, text, watermark.

SECTIUNEA 2 · IMAGINI EXECUTIVE PER ETAPA (6 imagini)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Cele 6 etape C09 (din BLUEPRINT-C09-RELATII.md):
1 REALITATE   - DESCHIDERE RELATIONALA (relatii doar identificate, inca inactive)
2 INVESTIGATIE- INTEROGARE ASISTATA DE AI (ce relatii pot fi activate, cardinalitate)
3 TRANSFORMARE- CONSTRUIREA MODELULUI (activeaza 1:M, model coerent)
4 VERIFICARE  - VERIFICAREA RELATIILOR (zero circulare, zero chei orfane)
5 STABILIZARE - ANCORARE LA SURSA (model viu, legat la surse)
6 CONFIRMARE  - PREDAREA MODELULUI (predat catre C10)

Fiecare imagine: 3:2 cinematic, acelasi stil Trainity, continuitate cu HERO.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXEC-STAGE 1 · REALITATE (DESCHIDERE RELATIONALA)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCOP PEDAGOGIC: arata punctul de plecare: sursele exista si harta lor e
cunoscuta (predata de C08), dar legaturile sunt doar IDENTIFICATE, nu active.
DESCRIERE VIZUALA: aceleasi placi de date separate, dar conectorii stau
decuplati alaturi; liniile dintre placi sunt punctate, reci, stinse (gri-albastru).
Nimic nu curge inca. Atmosfera de potential neactivat.
ELEMENTE OBLIGATORII: placi separate; conectori-cheie prezenti dar decuplati;
linii punctate stinse.
ELEMENTE INTERZISE: linii aprinse; fascicul de raspuns; harta difuza C08.
RELATIA CU AHA: aici esti la "date" - legaturile exista pe hartie, nu raspund.
PROMPT: Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2. Several separate brushed-steel
data slabs on matte black, arranged loosely apart. Between them, dotted cold
grey-blue guide lines mark where connections COULD be, but the machined
key-connectors lie uncoupled beside the slabs. Nothing glows, nothing flows;
quiet unrealized potential. Dramatic low-key side light, shallow depth of field,
graphite and steel-blue palette, no warm accent yet. 50mm f/2.0, photorealistic,
no text, no UI.
NEGATIVE PROMPT: glowing lines, amber light, answer beam, dashboard, charts,
ecosystem web, ranking, arrows, people, cartoon, text, watermark.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXEC-STAGE 2 · INVESTIGATIE (INTEROGARE ASISTATA DE AI)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCOP PEDAGOGIC: AI interogheaza ce relatii pot fi activate, ce cardinalitate
(1:1 vs 1:M) si ce chei sunt sigure. Inspectie, nu constructie inca.
DESCRIERE VIZUALA: un fascicul-scanner rece inspecteaza fittingul cheilor intre
doua placi; deasupra unei chei apare o eticheta gravata fina "1:M"; o cheie e
verificata, una e pusa deoparte ca nesigura.
ELEMENTE OBLIGATORII: scanner/fascicul de inspectie; chei examinate; semn de
cardinalitate 1:M gravat subtil.
ELEMENTE INTERZISE: relatii deja aprinse global; raspuns final; clasamente.
RELATIA CU AHA: inca nu ai raspunsuri; intrebi modelul ce poate fi legat.
PROMPT: Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2. A cold blue inspection beam scans
the precision key-connectors between two separate steel data slabs on matte
black. One key is held in the light and examined, a faint engraved label "1:M"
hovers near a fitting; another key is set aside as unsafe. Investigative,
diagnostic mood, no global glow yet, only the inspection beam. Shallow depth of
field, graphite and steel-blue palette, single cold accent. 50mm f/2.0,
photorealistic, no text overlay except the tiny engraved 1:M, no UI.
NEGATIVE PROMPT: full amber network, answer beam, dashboard, charts, ranking,
Pareto, cause arrows, buttons, alerts, people, cartoon, watermark.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXEC-STAGE 3 · TRANSFORMARE (CONSTRUIREA MODELULUI)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCOP PEDAGOGIC: momentul-cheie. Relatiile 1:M se activeaza, modelul coerent
prinde forma (star schema: fact central + dimensiuni legate).
DESCRIERE VIZUALA: cheile intra in lacasuri cu un click vizibil; liniile 1:M
se aprind una cate una in amber dinspre placa-fact spre fiecare dimensiune;
structura izolata devine un model curat, ordonat.
ELEMENTE OBLIGATORII: chei cuplate in lacas; linii 1:M aprinse amber; star
schema citibila (1 fact + dimensiuni).
ELEMENTE INTERZISE: masuri/cifre evidentiate (C10); clasament (C11); dashboard.
RELATIA CU AHA: treci de la "date" la structura care POATE raspunde.
PROMPT: Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2. The machined keys snap into their
sockets between a larger central steel slab (fact) and surrounding smaller slabs
(dimensions); amber relationship-lines ignite one by one from the center
outward, forming a clean ordered star pattern (one-to-many). The previously
isolated pieces become a coherent connected model. Decisive constructive mood,
dramatic side light, shallow depth of field, graphite palette with strong warm
amber accent on the newly active relations. 50mm f/2.0, photorealistic, no text,
no UI.
NEGATIVE PROMPT: highlighted numbers, named measures, KPI, ranking, leaderboard,
dashboard, charts, cause arrows, alerts, buttons, ecosystem cloud, people,
cartoon, watermark.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXEC-STAGE 4 · VERIFICARE (VERIFICAREA RELATIILOR)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCOP PEDAGOGIC: confrunta modelul: zero relatii circulare, cardinalitate
corecta, zero chei orfane care rup join-ul.
DESCRIERE VIZUALA: un model legat, calm; un singur conector-cheie ORFAN este
evidentiat: nu se potriveste in niciun lacas, ramane suspendat si marcat (rece,
respins), in timp ce restul relatiilor sunt aprinse si stabile.
ELEMENTE OBLIGATORII: model legat stabil; o cheie orfana evidentiata ca
neconforma; senzatie de control/verificare.
ELEMENTE INTERZISE: clasament; raspuns de business final; dashboard.
RELATIA CU AHA: un model care raspunde corect e un model fara chei rupte.
PROMPT: Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2. A connected steel data model with
stable amber one-to-many lines on matte black. Calm, controlled. One single
orphan key-connector is singled out: it fits no socket, held suspended in a thin
cold spotlight and clearly marked as rejected, while every other relation stays
lit and sound. Forensic verification mood. Shallow depth of field, graphite and
steel-blue palette, amber on the valid relations, cold accent on the rejected
key. 50mm f/2.0, photorealistic, no text, no UI.
NEGATIVE PROMPT: ranking, top bottom, Pareto, dashboard, charts, business
conclusion, cause arrows, alerts, buttons, ecosystem web, people, cartoon,
watermark.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXEC-STAGE 5 · STABILIZARE (ANCORARE LA SURSA)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCOP PEDAGOGIC: modelul devine viu si ancorat la surse: cand sursa se schimba,
relatiile raman; nu se reconstruieste de la zero.
DESCRIERE VIZUALA: modelul legat are acum ancore solide care coboara din
fiecare dimensiune spre placa-sursa de sub ea; relatiile pulseaza usor (viu);
o noua placa de sursa aluneca in jos si se prinde de aceleasi ancore fara sa
rupa modelul.
ELEMENTE OBLIGATORII: ancore sursa-model; relatii care raman la schimbarea
sursei; senzatie de permanenta/durabilitate.
ELEMENTE INTERZISE: buton de refresh ca UI; automatizare de flux (T5); dashboard.
RELATIA CU AHA: relatiile bune supravietuiesc sursei noi: raspunsul ramane.
PROMPT: Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2. The connected steel model now has
solid anchors descending from each dimension slab into a source plate beneath
it; the amber relation-lines pulse gently, alive. A fresh source plate slides in
from below and locks onto the same anchors without breaking the model.
Sense of durable, living structure anchored to its sources. Dramatic light,
shallow depth of field, graphite palette, steady amber accent. 50mm f/2.0,
photorealistic, no text, no UI, no buttons.
NEGATIVE PROMPT: refresh button, UI controls, automation flow, pipeline, robot,
dashboard, charts, ranking, cause arrows, alerts, people, cartoon, watermark.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXEC-STAGE 6 · CONFIRMARE (PREDAREA MODELULUI)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCOP PEDAGOGIC: modelul interogabil, complet si verificat, este predat catre
C10 (care va defini masuri peste el). C09 se inchide cu prima citire cross-tabel.
DESCRIERE VIZUALA: modelul star complet, aprins si stabil; un singur fascicul-
intrebare traverseaza doua placi si se intoarce cu un punct de raspuns; in
dreapta, un pod de predare se intinde spre o zona urmatoare goala, pregatita
(C10), inca neactivata.
ELEMENTE OBLIGATORII: model complet aprins; o singura citire cross-tabel
(fascicul + raspuns); pod de predare spre urmatoarea zona.
ELEMENTE INTERZISE: masuri numite in zona urmatoare; clasament; dashboard.
RELATIA CU AHA: dovada finala "cu relatii ai raspunsuri"; predai un model care
raspunde, nu doar date.
PROMPT: Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:2. The complete star data model fully
lit and stable on matte black. A single question-beam crosses two slabs and
returns carrying one bright amber answer-point (one cross-table read). To the
right, a clean handover bridge extends toward an empty, prepared next stage that
is not yet activated. Sense of completion and transfer. Dramatic side light,
shallow depth of field, graphite palette with confident amber accent. 50mm
f/2.0, photorealistic, no text, no UI.
NEGATIVE PROMPT: named measures in the next stage, numbers highlighted, ranking,
leaderboard, dashboard, charts, cause arrows, alerts, buttons, ecosystem cloud,
people, cartoon, watermark.
